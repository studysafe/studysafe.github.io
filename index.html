Looking to live sustainably?

Come to Earth Day Bengaluru!

What do we Do?

Earth Day Bengaluru is a one-of-a-kind event to bring the city together in living sustainably

What's Happening?

On the 22nd April 2018 at Arts Village Bengaluru, an amazing event is to take place- Earth Day Bengaluru! Here, we'll celebrate the joy of living sustainably through interactive workshops, stalls, and a variety of environmental products.

When?

Earth Day Bengaluru is to take place on the 22nd of April, 2018, from 10:30 AM to 19:00 PM. We hope to see you there!

What is our theme?

This year, Earth Day's main theme is plastic reduction and anti-waste. However, this doesn't mean that we'll focus on just that; other themes include:
• Water Wastage
• Clean environmental practices
• Fossil Fuels and Electricity
and much, much more!

Where can I find more info?

If you need any further information, please check out our social media pages; links are around the site!

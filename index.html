Useful Tips

Hydrate Yourself!

Studies have proven that appropriate hydration during revision sessions boosts concentration and memory. Make sure to eat plenty as well- maintaining a healthy diet when studying is the best you can do!

Keep Track

Maintain a close watch over the amount of time you're spending when revising- take breaks in between sessions and remove distractions in order to stay balanced and focused. Use our countdown timer above if you're feeling stuck.

Collaborate!

Collaborative learning can help a lot- why not invite some friends and revise together? Sharing facts is extremely helpful in written subjects such as English and History- but maintain your concentration!

Out of time? Go online!

Further information is available on plenty of websites, and BBC Bitesize contains loads of resources to refresh your memory. There are also loads of mental health links, which include:
• Mental Health Foundation UK
• MIND UK
• Patient Health Info
